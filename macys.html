About


Macy's had a huge problem regarding organization and visual clarity. My team was tasked with focusing on a specific goal of optimizing vendor content to increase the cohesive experience across the site. This lead us to exploring how to create a design system that would work inside the overall Macy's website, while allowing flexibility and accuracy for the art styles of various vendors. By working in a cross functioning team with UX and Marketing, we were able to successfully develop and implement a system that meets the target goals.


Role: User Testing, Design System, Design Strategy

Tools: Adobe XD, Photoshop, Chrome Inspector
