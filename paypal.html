About


I was fortunate to be an addition to the internal User Experience and Product Design team tasked with updating the look and feel of the PayPal Small Business and Enterprise redesign. The goal was to update the website, create new pages to attract new partnerships, while maintaining the spirit and vision of the brand. I assisted in quality assurance testing, checking for bugs and designing possible solutions while collaborating with content strategists and the PayPal front end engineering team to optimize and transform the site.


Role: Concept, Ideation, QA,
Design Tools: Figma, Sketch
